I am a freelance web and mobile app developer based in the greater New York City area. I enjoy creating amazing products and solving interesting problems.

This is what I do

I provide web and mobile application development services.

Web

Build responsive websites and web apps using the latest technologies. I have worked with multiple web frameworks such as Angular 4, ReactJS and NodeJS.

iOS

Build rich multi device iOS apps in the latest language Swift. My apps use some of the most popular mobile frameworks such as Firebase in order to build and launch quickly.

Android

All of our themes are crafted with care, and we strive to make your experience as one of our customers the best as possible.
